🎯 1. What This App Is For

The HR / Team Members app is one screen that shows your whole team. You can see each person, read their details, see which SOP training videos they have watched, add a new starter, and tick off who is trained on what.

Think of it as a smart address book joined to a training checklist. You open it in your web browser. It reads and saves everything in Airtable, so the data is always the real, live data.

ℹ️
Scope: In scope: viewing people, editing details, onboarding new starters, assigning and tracking SOP video training, logging achievements, and tracking reviews and handbook reads. Out of scope: payroll, payslips, and anything not in the Operations Director Airtable base.

💡 2. When To Use It

💬 Use the app when you want to
  • Look up a team member and see their full details.
  • Add a new starter and walk them through onboarding.
  • See who has watched which SOP video, and assign the ones they have not.
  • Mark someone as trained on a SOP.
  • Log a win, check a review date, or see who has read the handbook.
⚠️
Do not use the app for: paying people, sending contracts for signing (do that in Adobe), or any business outside Operations Director. For big bulk edits, work in Airtable directly.

1

Open the app and sign in

manual
  1. Find the file hr-team-members-app.html and double-click it. It opens in your browser.
  2. If it asks for a key, paste your Airtable key in the box and press the button.
  3. That is it. Next time on the same computer it opens straight to your team.
🔑
The key is like a password. Keep it private. If you do not have one, ask Kevin.
2

Find your way around the tabs

manual
  1. Overview: the home screen. It shows what needs doing today, like reviews due or onboarding in progress.
  2. Directory: a card for every team member. Search by name, or filter by status and department.
  3. Org Chart: shows who reports to whom.
  4. Training: the SOP training grid. More on this in step 5.
  5. Achievements: wins logged for each person.
  6. Reviews: who has a 6-month review coming up.
  7. Handbook: who has read the company handbook.
3

Open someone's profile

manual
  1. In Directory, click a person's card. A panel slides in from the right.
  2. It shows everything about them: personal details, contract, working days, pay details, businesses, SOPs assigned, reviews, achievements and handbook.
  3. At the top are three buttons: Edit, Open in Airtable, and Delete.
  4. Empty details show a dash, so you can see what is missing.
4

SOPs Assigned (on a profile)

manual
  1. Scroll the profile to SOPs Assigned. It lists the SOP videos that person is trained on or still watching.
  2. Tap any title to open and watch that SOP video.
  3. Press Assign a SOP to jump to the Training tab and add more.
  4. Press Manage in Airtable to add or remove SOPs on that record directly.
5

The Training matrix (the main workflow)

manual

This is the grid that shows who is trained on what. Rows are SOP videos. Columns are team members.

How to read a cell

  1. Green tick means trained.
  2. Amber half-circle means they are watching it now.
  3. Plus sign means a gap. They are not assigned yet.

What you can do

  1. Click a plus gap to assign that person to watch that SOP. The app makes the "watch this SOP" task for you.
  2. Click an amber cell to mark that person as trained once they have watched it.
  3. Use the filters to search a SOP, pick a department, show gaps only, or show all SOPs.
  4. The cards at the top show coverage and how many gaps are open.
🎓
The full loop: a SOP is created or a person joins a workflow, a watch task is assigned, they watch the video and finish the task, the cell ticks green, and the grid flags any gaps left to assign. There is a "How the training matrix works" panel at the top of the tab that explains this in five short steps.
6

Add a new starter (onboarding)

manual
  1. In Directory, press + New starter. Type their name and email.
  2. Press Open onboarding form, or Email form to starter to send it from Kevin's address.
  3. The new person fills the form. That creates their record with all the details.
  4. Open their profile. It now shows a 9-step onboarding checklist that ticks itself as each step is done.
  5. When every step is done, press Mark Active. They are now a full team member.
7

Edit, delete, and confirm

manual
  1. Press Edit on a profile to change details. The form has the same fields as the onboarding form.
  2. Press Save. The change is written to Airtable straight away.
  3. Anything risky, like Delete or Clear, asks "are you sure" first, so you cannot wipe something by accident.

🔧 5. Where The Data Lives

The app does not store anything by itself. It reads and writes to Airtable, so the data is always the real thing.

Place What it holds Status
Team Members table Every person and all their details. Connected
Video SOP system The SOP training videos and the list of who is trained on each one. Connected
Tasks table The "watch this SOP" tasks that show as Watching until done. Connected
Achievements table The wins logged for each person. Connected

✅ 6. Safety Checks

There is no sign-off step, but the app protects you in two ways.

⚠️
Are you sure: any delete, clear, mark trained, or assign action asks you to confirm before it happens. Read the message, then press confirm.
ℹ️
Live data: every save goes straight to Airtable. There is no draft. If you are not sure about a change, do not save it.

🚨 8. If Something Goes Wrong

  • "Failed to load": your key is wrong or has expired. Get a fresh key and paste it again.
  • A SOP line says "SOP" with no name: that SOP has no linked video record. Fix the link in Airtable, then refresh.
  • A change did not show: press the browser refresh button. The app reloads from Airtable.
  • A title will not open: that SOP has no Video Link yet. Add one in Airtable.

🛠️ 9. Quick Fixes

❌ The app asks for the key every time.
✅ Use the same browser, not private or incognito mode. The key is saved on that computer only.
❌ I assigned training but no amber dot showed.
✅ Wait a moment and refresh. The task needs to link to the person before it shows as Watching.
❌ The numbers or counts look off.
✅ Click another tab and back, or refresh the page, to reload the latest from Airtable.
❌ I deleted the wrong thing.
✅ Open the record in Airtable and restore it from there. The "are you sure" check is there to stop this, so read it next time.
